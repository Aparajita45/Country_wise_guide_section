USA

Visa Information:

F1 Student Visa required. Apply through SEVIS Interview and financial proof mandatory.

Top Universities:

  • Hardvard University
  • Stanford University
  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

Living Costs:

$1200-$1800/month (includes rent, food, transport)

Academic Requirements:

IELTS 6.5+, TOEFL 80+, GPA above 75%

UK

Visa Information:

Tier 4 (General) Student Visa. Apply online with CAS letter. Health surcharge required.

Top Universities:

  • University of Ocford
  • University of Cambridge
  • Imperial College London

Living Costs:

$1000-$1400/month (varies by city)

Academic Requirements:

IELTS 6.5+, 70-80% in previous academics

Canada

Visa Information:

Study Permit required. Must show proof of acceptance and financial capacity

Top Universities:

  • University of Toronto
  • University of British Columbia
  • McGill University

Living Costs:

CAD $1000-$1500/month

Academic Requirements:

IELTS 6.5+, TOEFL 86+, minimum 70% in academics

Australia

Visa Information:

Subclass 500 Student Visa. Apply with Conformation of Enrolment (CoE), OSHC required.

Top Universities:

  • University of Melbourne
  • Australian National University
  • University of Sydney

Living Costs:

AUD $1400-$1800/month

Academic Requirements:

IELTS 6.5+, GPA above 65%

Germany

Visa Information:

National Student Visa. Must show proof of admission and blocked account for funds

Top Universities:

  • Technical University of Munich
  • Heidelberg University
  • LMU Munich

Living Costs:

€800-€1000/month

Academic Requirements:

IELTS 6.0+, some programs require German proficiency, GPA 70%+
